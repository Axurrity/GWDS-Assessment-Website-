About us! Who we are, and what we do

We are Non-profit Italian Ristorante (restaurant) and cafe organization based in central London our aim is to help combat food waste created by the local major supermarkets and distributors in the London area. We operate a pay as you feel scheme meaning you as a customer decide the cost of your comodities. Ultimately our goal is to divert food waste into a sustainable restaurant situation where people can pay if they can, and if they are unable, they can still get a meal of nourishing food for themselves and their family.

About our food box plan where you can collect them and where you can find us

We also operate a pay as you feel food box, this food box is full of diverted fresh goods and food from the grocery stores and farmers markets located around the London area. These are available from select pick up community hubs located within the city on certain days (locations as seen on the map) If you would like to sign up and learn more please interact with the sign up button at the top of our home page. You can find us at 25 Millbank, London SW1P 4QP

How you can help our cause and get involved!

If you feel passionate about our cause and want to help or feel uncomfortable recieving food as a donation we also accept volunteer time on the floor, in the kitchen, and in the warehouse in exchange for food box collection! We also welcome monetary donations either in store at the time of service, or on-line. If interested please get in touch and email us at MM_Ristorante@gmail.com
